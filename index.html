A Tribute to The Greatest

""

Sir Kote,
the "ball junkie"

""

For you possess Beauty without Vanity,

Strength without Insolence,

Courage without Ferocity,

and all the virtues of Man without his Vices.

These words are for you, my dearest friend
Just a dog, some dared they say.
But they don’t know you like we do,
ever the grateful, ever the pure.

It’s from afar that you can tell,
stranger steps from foe or friend.
Steady the paw mighty the bark,
yet those who love you know you won’t bite.

Mankind's problem you understand,
seeking purpose every step of the land.
Your calling is clear, almost God-given,
to provide love to those who’ve been striven.

So many years I long by your side,
to chase the ball, from dawn until dusk.
I know that many will not comprehend,
it’s with great honour I call you my friend.
